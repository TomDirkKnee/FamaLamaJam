## Manual-Only Verification

The phase should close with a short Ableton smoke matrix covering:

- local strip toggles into voice mode with orange state and `Voice mode ready`
- transmit on in voice mode sends audible near-realtime voice
- switching back to interval mode returns through interval warmup before green
- remote voice peer appears as an orange voice strip in the normal mixer
- mixed room with at least one interval peer and one voice peer works at the same time
